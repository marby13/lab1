Low Energy Firmware & Hardware for a Wearable Health Tracker

Background
Bluetooth Low-Energy (BLE), also know as Bluetooth Smart, has become a core technology in the world of mobile devices. BLE has enabled the expansion of wearable sensors and wireless connectivity.

Objective
To develop firmware for an extended range Bluetooth Low-Energy module (BLE121LR) to collect and process data from sensors, and relay them to a compatible Android device.

Conclusion
Successfully designed firmware to communicate sensor data via the BLE module to an Android device. In addition, we designed and fabricated a prototype printed circuit board.

Approach & Methodology

  • Learn BGScript programming.
  • Research sensors.
  • Connect sensors to BLE module.
  • Create Android interface.
  • Design a prototype circuit.
  • Convert raw data to common units.
